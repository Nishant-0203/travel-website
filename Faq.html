FREQUENT ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need relevant experience in the travel industry?

Experience in the travel industry is preferred but is not a show stopper for people who have passion for the travel industry, adequate business acumen, leadership traits, proven management capabilities, and an ability to adapt to a constantly changing and evolving travel industry is preferred.

MakeMyTrip is an online company, so what is expected from the retail stores?

Retail stores are opened to cater to the increasing holiday needs of the customers. Generally, it is seen that customers prefer to book holidays post a satisfactory and thorough discussion in person with executives at our stores, as compared to making an online booking.

What is the potential for earnings?

Franchisee gets a fixed commission on fixed departures holidays, a percentage based commission on different customized products of holidays and hotels.
